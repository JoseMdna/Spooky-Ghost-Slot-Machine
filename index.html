How to Play:

A slot machine game where you can play as a guest or create your own account to save your balance, both options are guaranteed to be a fun experience! Every player starts with a balance of 100$ and is able to bet up their current balance or as low as 0.01$. The game ends once the guest user's balance hits 0.00$, the users who create an account also get the additional option to reload their balance back to 100$ when they hit 0$, or save the game and logout.
