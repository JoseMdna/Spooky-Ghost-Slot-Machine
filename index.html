Welcome to My Spooky Ghost Slot Machine Game!

A game where high risk leads to high rewards!

How to Play:

This is a slot machine game where you can either play as a guest or create an account to save your balance. Both options guarantee a fun experience! Every player starts with a balance of $100 and can place bets as high as their current balance or as low as $0.01.

The game ends when a guest user's balance reaches $0.00. However, users who create an account have the additional option to reload their balance to $100 when it hits $0.00 or save their progress snd log out.
